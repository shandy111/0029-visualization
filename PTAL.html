Public Transport Accessibility (PTAL) & EV Charging Does better public transport mean more or fewer chargers?

About PTAL

PTAL (Public Transport Accessibility Level) measures how well an area is served by public transport, ranging from 1 (poor) to 6b (excellent). Areas with high PTAL tend to have lower car dependency — but does this mean fewer or more public EV chargers?
